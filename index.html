Who we are?

Vidya Pay works to ease out the hassles of Education Tution fee payments. Currently in most of the schools parents have to personally visit the schools and colleges to pay their ward's Tution fee. This costs working professional valuable time on their working days. With the nation going cashless this can change. We strive to provide online fee payment gateway setup services to Educational Institutes and providing them technical support in boosting their online presence. Our mission is to take the Education Sector forward in the national drive to go cashless. We work to Simplify the financial transactions that occur in the form of Tution Fee. We envision to support Educational Institutions in bringing them online and closer to students and their parents in Digital India.
